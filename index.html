We have set up multiple IEEE awards for PRDC 2023: Distinguished papers in the main track will receive the 2023 IEEE Distinguished Contribution Award on Dependable Computing. Distinguished papers in the industry track will receive the 2023 IEEE Distinguished Industrial Contribution Award on Dependable Computing. Distinguished papers in the fast abstracts track will receive the 2023 IEEE Distinguished Visionary Poster Award on Dependable Computing.
01/Apr/2023(News)
We have confirmed fast-tracking and special issue publications with top journals in the field. Top accepted papers at PRDC 2023 that are related to security will be invited to be extended and published at the IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing (TDSC). Top papers related to formal methods will be invited to be extended and published at the Formal Aspects of Computing (FAC).

About

IEEE PRDC 2023 is the twenty-eight event in the series of symposia started in 1989 that are devoted to dependable and fault-tolerant computing. PRDC is recognized as the main event in the Pacific area that covers many dimensions of dependability and fault tolerance, encompassing fundamental theoretical approaches, practical experimental projects, and commercial components and systems. As applications of computing systems have permeated into all aspects of daily life, the dependability of computing system has become increasingly critical. This symposium provides a forum for countries around the Pacific Rim and other areas of the world to exchange ideas for improving the dependability of computing systems.
